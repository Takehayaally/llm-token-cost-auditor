# FAQ

## Does this connect to my provider account?

No. It only reads local JSONL logs that you provide.

## Are prices official?

No. The pricing file is editable because model prices change. Always update `examples/pricing.json` with your current provider rates.

## What is the paid bundle for?

The paid bundle is for teams that want rule presets, batch examples, and packaged reporting templates.

## Refund boundary

Refunds are reasonable when the included examples do not run as described. Private log review, accounting advice, and provider billing disputes are not included.
